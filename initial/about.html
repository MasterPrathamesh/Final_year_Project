Welcome to Neural Diagnosis

Exploring the Power of Neural Networks for Eye Diagnosis

Our Mission

This project aims to leverage advanced neural networks to enhance the early detection and diagnosis of eye diseases. Our goal is to make eye care accessible, efficient, and highly accurate, addressing the global challenge of rising vision impairments.

How It Works

This project involves:

  1. Training multiple neural networks on retinal image datasets
  2. Evaluating and comparing model performance
  3. Deploying best-performing models

Why This Research?

Eye diseases like diabetic retinopathy and glaucoma often go undetected until it's too late. Manual screening is time-consuming. Our AI solutions enable early detection and reduce healthcare burden.

Applications

Key applications include:

  • Automated diagnosis tools
  • Telemedicine integration
  • Personalized treatment planning
